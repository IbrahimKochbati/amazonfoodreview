Kopali's chocolate covered bananas are amazing!  The bananas are naturally sun dried, so they are chewy, which makes them different from most chocolate covered bananas.  And the chocolate is vegan, gluten free, and Fair Trade!  I couldn't ask for anything more in a food product!  I also love putting these on top of vanilla ice cream as a nice alternative to more unhealthy toppings.  Nice work, Kopali!
