I used to eat a lot of this when I first "discovered" the pleasure of oatmeal about a year ago. Now, I make my own using Quaker's "Old Fashioned Oats," (or Target's cheaper equivalent), which takes only 10 minutes or so, allows me to control the sweetness to my liking by adding the maple syrup and  own sugar myself, and doesn't give me any "artificial flavors" or "guar gum," or "caramel color."          Given that, I will say that, for an instant product, this is pretty good, though just a bit too sweet for me. Its first ingredient is Whole Grain Rolled Oats (although the second is sugar) and it does have a reasonably creamy consistency which you would expect from oatmeal.
