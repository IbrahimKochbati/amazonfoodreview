These strawberry gummies have just the right flavor and are a great chew.     This 5 pound bag goes a long way and is full of flavor.     If you are into baking, they make a nice decoration for the top of cupcakes or a cake.     These candies are eye pleasers complete with strawberry looking green tops and raised places on the strawberry denoting seeds.  Kids will love them because they are cute as well as tasty.
