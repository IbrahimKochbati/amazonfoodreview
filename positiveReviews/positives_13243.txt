Wild Strawberry, Raspberry Lemonade, Lemonade, and Cherry Pomegranate are the flavors. Each is delicious and the price is unbeatable. Unlike other  ands I've tried, like 4C, I've never gotten a packet where the powder has clumped up or been stubborn about mixing well inside the bottle when shaken. The powder distributes evenly within the bottle for a great on the go drink.
