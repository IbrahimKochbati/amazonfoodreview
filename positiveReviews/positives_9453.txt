Do you love candy, but are tired of all that sugar? Well, Werther's Original has a "Sugar Free" version that is the best. Unlike most sugar free candy, these taste exactly like their sugar cousins and there is absolutely no after taste   none. You don't realize that they are sugar free unless someone tells you they are. Perfect for diabetics who want a snack but can't handle the sugar. Keep a package handy and you'll not be sorry.
