I LOVE ripe green olives, but do not often buy them, because I cannot eat less than a can at a time.          Lindsay olives are what they are:  Very distinct from typical American stuffed queen olives.  No vinegar.  Only slightly salty.  With a mild nutty flavor.  To me, they most closely resemble ripe black olives   but milder.          If you like olives but are on a low salt diet, and perhaps mostly use them in salads, or even sliced on pizza, Lindsay Olives are terrific, as is the price.
