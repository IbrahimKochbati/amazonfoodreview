Besides black and red pepper this and cumin are my most used spices. I always sprinkle some over basmati rice, which is daily staple, as well as other dishes. It goes perfectly with fish.  When I have guests over and fish is on the menu, I use a little of this as a rub for guests who do not like fish that tastes fishy (go figure!)          Other dishes that this goes perfectly with as a table spice are  occoli and rapini. I sometimes will sprinkle it on salads as well.
