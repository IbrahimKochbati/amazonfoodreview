If you have dry skin, give this a try. A doctor recommended I try this when I complained of itchy skin due to dryness. It's definitely more moisturizing than the bar soaps I used to use.          As someone already mentioned, the packaging design is horrible. I used to keep almost empty bottles propped upside down in my shower caddy, but then decided to use a pump bottle instead, which I refill.
