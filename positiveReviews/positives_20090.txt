For those of you who don't know Pork Roll, it is a staple of diners and boardwalk grills in Jersey. It's taste is unique. Grill 3 slices and put them on a hard roll with cheese and or an onion slice and prepare to be transported. For  eakfast take out the onion and add a hard fried egg and a dash of ketchup and use an English muffin.
